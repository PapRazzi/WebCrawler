The Gamasutra Network's RSS Feeds
The Gamasutra Network, headed by our flagship site on the art and business of making games, Gamasutra.com, also includes a number of other notable sister websites. These deal with subjects such as game education (GameCareerGuide.com), online games (WorldsInMotion.biz), iPhone games (FingerGaming.com), indie games (IndieGames.com), digitally downloaded console/handheld games (GamerBytes.com),'serious' games (SeriousGamesSource.com), mobile games (GamesOnDeck.com) and more. The parent company of Gamasutra, UBM TechWeb, also operates events in the Game Developers Conference series.
