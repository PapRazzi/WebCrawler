Must-Have Accessories for a Party

Dressing Up for a Party With Accessories

From Kori Ellis, former About.com Guide

See More About:
  • must-have accessories
  • holiday accessories
  • diamond earrings
  • cocktail rings
These must-have accessories should be staples in your wardrobe. With these items, you will be ready for any special occasion, from a holiday party to friend's wedding to a formal gala. Mix and match these accessories with your favorite little black dress or formal gown to find the perfect outfit for any party.

Diamond Earrings

Courtesy of Netaya
Every woman should have one pair of diamond earrings, even if it's a simple pair of studs. If you can't afford real diamonds, don't worry. Get cubic zirconia and no one will even notice. If you are going to pull up your hair, a pair of dangling drop earrings with diamonds, cubic zirconia or sparkling crystals will look fantastic.
Buy Direct

Bold Necklace

Courtesy of J.Crew
A long necklace or a few necklaces in varying lengths will add a touch of shine to any dress. If you are looking for some color, pick a chunky necklace in a shorter length to draw attention to your face.
Vendor's Site

Cocktail Ring

Courtesy of Nordstrom
A big cocktail ring doesn't have to be expensive to make a statement. Choose a large colorful center stone surrounded by rhinestones or other crystals.
Buy Direct

Strappy Silver Sandals

Courtesy of Heels.com
To add a little glitz to your look, a sexy pair of strappy sandals in silver are the perfect option. Silver works with just about any color. You can wear them with your LBD, a sequined mini-dress or even a full-length formal gown.
Buy Direct

Black Patent Slingbacks

Courtesy of Zappos
If the silver sandals don't seem appropriate, then put on a glossy pair of patent slingbacks. These black shoes will go with just about anything in your closet.
Buy Direct

Beaded Clutch

Courtesy of Endless
A beaded clutch handbag is a definite must for any party. Pick a small clutch that isn't so small that it's useless. It should be big enough to hold your cell phone, lipstick, compact, driver's license and keys.
Buy Direct

Sheer Wrap

Courtesy of Neiman Marcus
On cold evenings, don't cover up your party dress with a coat. Instead add a sheer wrap to provide a little coverage and still let the beauty of your outfit shine through.
Vendor's Site
