Essential Winter Accesories

Must-Have Accessories for Winter

From Kirsten Nunez, former About.com Guide

See More About:
  • winter accessories
  • shop for accessories
  • winter fashion
As fall turns into winter, prepare yourself with this list of must-have winter accessories. Even if you live in an area that barely gets snow, these accessories will help you stay warm on the more chilly days of the year. With these essential winter accessories, you’ll be able to complete your cold weather wardrobe of thick sweaters, knee high boots and peacoats.

1. Knit Beanies

FarFetch.com
When the temperatures start to drop, keep your ears with a cozy beanie. The fitted, snug style of a beanie is wonderful for keeping the cold air out. These hats tend to be more appropriate for casual looks, but can look more sophisticated when made with knit fabric in tasteful colors.

2. Earmuffs

Topshop.com
If you'd like a dressier alternative to keep your ears warm, you can't go wrong with a stylish pair of earmuffs. Often made with faux fur, they'll add a touch of luxury to your ensemble. Faux fur options will also be much more affordable than earmuffs made with real fur. They look especially chic with peacoats in vibrant colors, such as red or purple.

3. Felt Floppy Hat

Nordstrom.com
Contrary to what you may think, floppy hats aren't just for the summertime. Wide brimmed hats made of felt -- or even wool -- are a unique way to top off your winter outfits. Like earmuffs, they're a great alternative for dressier looks.

4. Black Leather Gloves

Oasis-stores.com
As one of the accessories that every woman should own, it goes without saying that black leather gloves are an essential accessory for the cooler season. Timeless and classic, you'll be able to wear them during each and every winter. Consider investing in a nice, well made pair that will last you for years to come.

5. Glitter Accessories

Nordstrom.com
Nothing sparkles more than a holiday party in the middle of winter. It's also the best time to adorn your outfits with glitter accessories, a style that is more than appropriate for this time of the year. Gold and silver are the most festive shades, but you really can't go wrong with any color. Glitter accessories are also excellent for ringing in the new year. From jewelry to bags, check out my list of top picks in glitter accessories.

6. Metallic Accessories

Forever21.com
Wearing accessories in metallic shades is another way to shine during the winter season. Stay sophisticated by adding just a small touch of metallic to your outfit. Items such as skinny belts, stud earrings or headbands are all perfect choices for accessorizing with metallic shades.

7. Cable Knit Tights

HM.com
During the winter season, you can make the most out of your favorite skirts and dresses by wearing them with a pair of cable knit tights. Layering is the best way to keep warm and stylish on those chilly days. You can even wear them under jeans or pants for ultimate protection from the cold.

8. Oversized Blanket Scarf

Buckle.com
Oversized blanket scarves are great accessories for layering during the cooler months. They are much larger than normal scarves, allowing you to wear them in different ways. You can wrap it around your neck like usual, or wrap it around yourself. Another option is to drape it on both your shoulders, creating the look of a loose flowing cardigan.

9. Fur Trapper Hat

HM.com
For ultimate warmth, try wearing a fur trapper hat. These are usually lined with soft fur, making them amazingly comfortable to wear. The exterior is also usually made with water proof material, making it ideal for the snowiest of days.

10. Convertible Mittens

AE.com
Protecting your hands from the cold is an absolute must during the winter. With a pair of convertible mittens or gloves, you can cover up your fingers with ease. Thanks to the fabric flap, things like eating, driving, or using a cell phone will be so much easier.
