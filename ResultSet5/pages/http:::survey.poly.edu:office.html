Overview

Welcome to Polytechnic Institute of NYU's Office of Assessment and Institutional Research Homepage. This website is designed to serve as a resource on outcomes assessment for Polytechnic's faculty, administration and students.

If you explore this Web Site, you'll discover information on assessment, online surveys, reports, workshops, and library resources. Under Assessment Resources/Documents you'll find links to professional organizations, other institutional assessment sites and any online resources we have.

The Poly community can click on Course Evaluations or Online Surveys to complete current assessment forms online.
