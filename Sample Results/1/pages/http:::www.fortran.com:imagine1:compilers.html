F Compilers



The F compiler is accessed by typing 

     g95 -std=F my_program.f95
where g95 is the free Fortran 95 compiler available at http://www.g95.org.

g95 is also available as a component of the Fortran Tools. The package contains CDs with the compiler, Photran IDE, lots of example code, three books in electronic form, other software tools, and the Fortran Tools manual in electronic form.

You have the option to order a book when you order a Fortran Tools CD, but you also may order one separately.

The Fortran Tools cost $49 and may be ordered from the Fortran Store.
