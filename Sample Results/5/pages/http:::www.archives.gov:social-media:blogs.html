Records Management and Web 2.0

  • Guidance on Managing Records in Web 2.0/Social Media Platforms
  • Web 2.0 Discussion on Records Express Blog

The National Archives maintains several blogs in the interest of sharing more information with the public about our activities. They also exist as a channel for the public to respond to news, upcoming events, and proposed projects. We encourage your input, but please be aware of our policies concerning comments on our blogs.

You can visit our blogs by clicking on the titles or subscribe through the RSS options below each link.
