Anne L. Fritz, About.com's Guide to 15-Minute Fashion, has been covering the fashion and beauty world since 1994.

Experience:

Anne worked in the magazine industry for over a decade where she learned all her ingenious tips and tricks for looking good in 15 minutes or less. Before starting her freelance career and her website The Jet Set Girls, Anne was the Style Director at Life & Style Weekly and the Fashion & Beauty Editor at Woman's Day. Anne lives in New York City, where an average day could take her from a business meeting to a product launch to cocktails at the latest seen-and-be-seen restaurant, with no time to go home and change in between. Anne prides herself on her ability to get dressed in 15 minutes or less, whatever the occasion.

Education:

Anne L. Fritz earned her bachelor's degree from Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications in magazine journalism.

From Anne Fritz:

Like you, I’m balancing my career, my relationship and my home, not to mention I try to stay fit and healthy. I also like to make time to do the things I enjoy, including spending time with my girlfriends and traveling. As a busy woman on the go I don’t have 30 minutes plus to spend getting dressed everyday, but I still want to look my best. It's important; I strongly believe that when a woman looks good, she feels better. Fortunately, through my years as a beauty and fashion editor, I’ve learned many tips and tricks to help me get dressed quickly. Now, I would like to share them with you.
