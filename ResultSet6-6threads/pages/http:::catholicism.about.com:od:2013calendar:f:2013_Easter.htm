Question: When Is Easter 2013?
Easter 2013, the greatest feast day in the Christian 2013 calendar, celebrates the Resurrection of Jesus Christ. What is the date of Easter 2013?
Answer: Easter Sunday falls on March 31, 2013.

For the date of Easter in other years, see When Is Easter? For the date of Eastern Orthodox Easter 2013, see When Greek Orthodox Easter Is Celebrated (from the About.com GuideSite to Greece Travel).

For the dates of other Holy Days of Obligation and moveable feasts associated with Easter 2013, see the Catholic 2013 calendar.
