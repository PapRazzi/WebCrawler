About The Love Calculator

nomen est omen

The Love Calculator is a program written by Thijs Kinkhorst and Matthijs Sypkens Smit. At first, it had been written in JavaScript. Because not all browsers support JavaScript, we decided to create a Perl version. The Love Calculator has been online since November 1996. Since then, it has had millions visitors. It has appeared television and radio shows and was featured in magazines and newspapers. The Love Calculator has won many awards.

Privacy
No personally identifyable information is collected by The Love Calculator about its visitors. Specifically, the names entered for calculations are not stored in any way. more
