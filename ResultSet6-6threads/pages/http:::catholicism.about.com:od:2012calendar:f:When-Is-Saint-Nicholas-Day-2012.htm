Question: When Is Saint Nicholas Day 2012?
Saint Nicholas Day celebrates the life of Saint Nicholas of Myra, a fourth-century bishop best known today as the real-life model for Santa Claus. What is the date of Saint Nicholas Day 2012?
Answer: Saint Nicholas Day falls on Thursday, December 6, 2012.
