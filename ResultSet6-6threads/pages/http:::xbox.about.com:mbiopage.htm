Eric Qualls has been covering the Xbox and Xbox 360 for About.com since August 2004 and has eight years of professional experience covering the videogame industry.

Experience:

In addition to his work at About.com, Eric has been an editor at gaming website www.gamesfirst.com and has also done freelance work for the Ziff-Davis magazine GMR. In addition to his professional experience, Eric has also contributed work to various fansites including PDark.com and Ninten.com.

From Eric Qualls:

I have been playing videogames for nearly twenty years, and writing about them as a hobby and then as a job for about half that time. And even after all of the work and hundreds upon hundreds of games I have played, I am still a rabid fan of videogames. I still get excited about new releases, and my goal is to try and get everyone else just as excited as I am about the Xbox 360 and videogames in general.
