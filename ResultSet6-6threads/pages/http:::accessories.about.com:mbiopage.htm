Kori Ellis loves accessories, fashion and style. She aims to use her fashion knowledge to help you find the perfect accessories for any outfit from an elegant evening dress to a T-shirt and jeans.

Experience:

Kori is a freelance writer and editor. She has been writing professionally for many years and has hundreds of articles published worldwide in magazines, newspapers and websites. She's the Senior Editor for b5media, where she manages more than 100 bloggers and writes several blogs herself. Kori also contributes regularly to the Beauty & Style channel at SheKnows.com.

From Kori Ellis:

Fashion is my passion. I hope to provide you with a wealth of information on handbags, sunglasses, hats and other fashion accessories. Here at About.com, you can find my fashion tips and shopping suggestions on what to wear and how to choose accessories on any budget.
