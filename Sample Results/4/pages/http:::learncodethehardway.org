    <div class="column-220">
        <h3>The LxTHW Philosophy</h3>
        <p>"Less Talk, More Code" summarizes the philosophy.  By having students
        get code working <em>first</em>, and explaining it second, you cut down
        on much of the difficulty of explaining programming concepts to the 
        uninitiated.</p>
        <p>A LxTHW book simply has students do exercises, focuses on practice and 
        rote repetition, and instills an attitude that to learn anything one must
        be prepared to work at it.
        </p>
