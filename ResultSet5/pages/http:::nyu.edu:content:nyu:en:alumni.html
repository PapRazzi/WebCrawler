NYU alumni are a community of nearly 420,000 people living and working around the world and representing the viewpoints, languages, and cultures of the global network university. From this site, alumni can access information on benefits, news, events, and connect with fellow alumni.

The NYU Alumni Card is given to alumni who make an annual gift to NYU in grateful acknowledgment of their financial support of NYU and its mission and provides three visits to Bobst Library, one visit to Jerome S. Coles & Palladium, discounts at the NYU Bookstores, access to the NYU Torch Club, discounted tickets at the Skirball Center, campus transportation, and Ticket Central discounts.

Be sure to stay up to date on alumni news, events, and information by liking our Facebook page, following us on Twitter, and joining our LinkedIn group.
