Publications

The Department of Computer Science produces a large number of research papers, books and technical reports every year, most of which can be found and downloaded from these pages. You can either find details of the publication from an author's personal page, or you can search for it directly here.

Technical reports

  • Computer Science by year from 2003
  • Monographs by year from 1970 to 2000. Published by The Programming Research Group. These were printed as A5 booklets but are now out-of-print.
  • Research reports from 2001 to 2008
