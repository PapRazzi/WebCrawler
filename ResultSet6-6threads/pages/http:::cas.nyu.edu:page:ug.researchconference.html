Undergraduate Research Conference

39th Annual Undergraduate Research Conference

Friday, April 12, 2013

The College takes seriously the role of research in the liberal arts education of undergraduates.  Students regularly benefit from direct contact with our research faculty and are empowered and encouraged to conduct their own inquiries.  All undergraduates at the University are invited to participate in and present their research at the Collegeâ€™s 39th annual Undergraduate Research Conference on Friday, April 12, 2013. Projects showcasing undergraduate work in the Natural Sciences, Social Sciences and Humanities are welcome. The work may have been done in connection with a course or as independent or supervised study.

Applications to participate in the Conference are available online.

All Undergraduate Research Conference presenters are strongly encouraged to attend a workshop (dates and times TBA) entitled "Designing and Delivering Dynamic Presentations." Click here for slides from past workshops.
